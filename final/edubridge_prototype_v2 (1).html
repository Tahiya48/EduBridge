About this Scholarship

The National Merit Scholarship supports outstanding students from low-income families to pursue higher education at any accredited Malaysian university. The award covers tuition fees, monthly living allowance, and annual book grants.

Eligibility Criteria
✓
Malaysian citizen, aged 18–25
✓
Minimum CGPA of 3.50 or equivalent
✓
Household income below RM 4,000/month
✓
Enrolled in approved degree program
✗
Not holding any other scholarship
